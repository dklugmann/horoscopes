1) Copy the horoscopes.zip file to the Wordpress Plugins directory , wp-content/plugins.

2) Unzip the horoscopes.zip file. This should create a new plugin directory called Horoscopes

3) Login to Wordpress Admin and click on Plugins and the new plugin Horoscopes should appear in the list. Click on Activate.

4) Click on Settings / Horoscopes and fill in the parameters, Website URL , Contact Email and Number of Months Displayed and then Save the Options.

5) On the page and place where you want the horoscopes displayed insert the keyword [horoscopes]

Reports are usually available for the next month around the 25th of the preceeding month.
